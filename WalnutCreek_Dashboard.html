The Safety Story: 2015–2019 vs 2020–2024

Equal 5-year periods · Pre vs Post-COVID · All stats normalized for fair comparison
📈
KSI Rate Surged Post-COVID
+61.5% KSI rate
KSI rate jumped from 4.47% (2015–19) to 7.22% (2020–24) — a 61.5% increase despite a 23.2% drop in total crashes. Walnut Creek faces a severe post-COVID severity crisis: fewer crashes but each one is dramatically more deadly.
🛣️
Ygnacio Valley Rd: Chronic Danger Corridor
5.3% KSI rate
Ygnacio Valley Rd is the clear #1 corridor across both eras with 25 total KSI (10-yr). Era A: 10 KSI · Era B: 15 KSI. The one existing HSIP project (C12, $1.92M) addressed protected left turns — but the broader corridor still lacks LPI, lighting upgrades, and ped treatments.
🚶
Pedestrian Safety: Acute Crisis
21.6% ped KSI rate
Pedestrian KSI rate was 12.2% pre-COVID and 21.6% post-COVID. 21 ped KSI post-COVID = 19% of all KSI. 36% of ped KSI occur in dark conditions — lighting and HAWK signals are the priority.
🔀
HIN Expanding: 4 New Danger Corridors
4 new streets
The HIN gained 4 newly-dangerous corridors post-COVID while 5 cooled. Only 8 corridors persist across both periods. Walnut Creek's danger pattern has shifted citywide post-COVID, requiring reassessment of all corridors.
🍺
Alcohol: 3.7× Deadlier Than Average
17.4% KSI rate
Alcohol crashes carry a 17.4% KSI rate vs 4.7% non-alcohol — 3.7× more deadly. Alcohol KSI rate rose from 13.7% to 20.7% post-COVID. Late night (0–5 AM) KSI rate hits 17.3% — 3.9× midday.
⚠️
Fatalities: Rising Concern
1.8 → 2.0/yr
Fatalities held steady from 1.8/yr to 2.0/yr (9 pre-COVID vs 10 post-COVID). With only 112 total KSI over 10 years, each fatality represents a major share of citywide harm.
🌙
Dark + Alcohol: Compound Lethality
22.1% combined rate
Alcohol + dark: KSI rate hits 22.1% — 3.1× city average. Weekend nights (Fri–Sun after 9 PM): 11.6% KSI rate. 36% of pedestrian KSI occur in dark conditions — lighting on Ygnacio Valley Rd is critical.
🚗💨
Hit Object: Growing Problem
+14% volume
Hit Object crashes moved from 15.8/yr to 18.0/yr with a KSI rate of 16.7%. Fixed-object hazard audits along Ygnacio Valley Rd, N. Main St, and North California Blvd are warranted.
⚠️
HSIP Track Record: Only 1 Project, Large Gap
$1.92M (C12 only)
Walnut Creek has received just one HSIP project in history — C12 protected left-turn phasing on Ygnacio Valley Rd at 4 intersections ($1.92M). No set-aside applications (SA-Ped, SA-Bike) have ever been submitted. With a 61.5% KSI rate increase, C13 is urgent.
